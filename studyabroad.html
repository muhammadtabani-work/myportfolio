Project 3 : Study Abroad In Japan

July 2023 - August 2023

Let's Connect!

During the summer of 2023, I was given the opportunity to study Abroad in Japan, through UTA.

About the class and projects:

The total duration of the travel photography class was 5 weeks. The first week was on campus at UTA and the following 4 weeks were in Japan.


Tasks:

  • Capture 150 photos linked to a theme which was provided by the instructor daily.

  • Flag my top 20 photos out of the 150.

  • Pick my top 5 photos from the 20 Flaged photos.

  • Edit the 5 top photos using Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom.

  • Write a short 150 word essay on highlights of what I learned during the day.

  • Using the daily essays and photos, create a site using StoryMaps highlighting the project for the week.
Visit External Site →

Class outcome:

At the conclusion of the five-week period, my experiences and high-quality images were showcased across three distinct StoryMaps sites.


What I learned:

  • The Japanese Culture.
  • Maximized the capabilities of my iPhone camera.
  • Japanese History.
  • Editing photos proficiently using Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom.
